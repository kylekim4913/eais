This poster is a modular grid-based composition featuring symbolic geometric forms in flat, high-contrast colors. The motifs suggest motion, repetition, and balance, aligning with the accompanying text “SWIM / BIKE / RUN.” It resembles a visual identity system or event branding design, emphasizing clarity, movement, and modern athleticism.

Line

Lines are curved, clean, and uniform, forming concentric rings, wavy flows, and circular cuts.
No expressive or sketch-like lines—everything is digitally precise.
Line thickness varies across modules, creating visual rhythm.

Shape

Composition is built entirely from abstract geometric shapes: circles, waves, and slices.
Shapes are isolated in each grid unit, forming distinct visual icons.
Shape repetition supports the themes of cycle and discipline.

Form

Entirely flat, with no shading or illusion of depth.
Form exists purely in silhouette, relying on shape arrangement and contrast.
No modeling—form is graphic and symbolic.

Color

Limited triadic palette: orange, blue (in multiple saturations), and white.
Occasional use of pale green adds variety.
High saturation and clear division between colors enhance readability and sports energy.

Value

Strong value contrast between light and dark blues, orange, and white.
Value variation helps separate foreground and background in each unit.
Effective for maintaining balance and visual tempo.

Texture

Smooth, vector-style finish with no implied surface texture.
Uniform flatness across modules gives the design a clean, modern identity.
Texture is absent, supporting the minimalist aesthetic.

Space

Flat grid structure provides a rigid spatial framework.
Each quadrant isolates its content, creating mini-compositions.
Negative space is well-managed to avoid clutter and enhance symbol clarity.
